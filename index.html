Why play?

A tailored experience for those survival game enthusiasts who seek unforgiving gameplay, replayability and realistic FPS mechanics.

Hardcore

Advanced mechanics, no fantasy-elements and a mysterious high-risk, high-reward permadeath zone.

Immersion

Based on real-world abandoned locations to ensure a unique and authentic post-apocalyptic atmosphere.

Transparency

Realistic milestones, public demos, devlogs and a continuous feedback loop.
